Human touches in this design:

Asymmetric cards

Each feature card has different content types — one has an accent bar, one has a quote, one has tags, one has a big number. Not cookie-cutter.

Imperfect details

The underline beneath the headline is slightly tilted. The "you belong here" annotation is casual and off-angle. Small things that feel hand-placed.

Varied stat labels

"Resources curated" not just "Resources". "Mentors ready to help" not just "Mentors". Each stat reads like someone wrote it, not generated it.

Mixed content density

The map section is dense with data. The hero is spacious. The pioneer spotlight is a single focused moment. Rhythm varies like a real editorial layout.
