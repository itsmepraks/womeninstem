# Code of conduct

This is a small project. A few expectations cover most situations.

**Be kind.** Disagree with ideas, not people. The audience here is young women exploring STEM — the tone of every interaction should reflect that.

**Be honest.** Don't submit fake resources, inflated claims, or data you can't verify. If you suggest a scholarship, you should have clicked the link yourself.

**Assume good faith.** Most mistakes are mistakes. Ask before accusing.

**Keep it on-topic.** Issues and PRs are for work related to the directory. Political arguments, off-topic debates, or unrelated self-promotion get closed.

## What isn't tolerated

- Harassment, personal attacks, or slurs directed at any contributor or visitor
- Doxxing or sharing someone's private information
- Submitting deliberately misleading resources (scam scholarships, fake organizations)
- Aggressive or persistent pressure after a maintainer declines a contribution

## Reporting

If something happens that needs attention, email [hello@praks.me](mailto:hello@praks.me). Reports stay private. Responses happen within a few days.

Violations may result in comments being edited or removed, issues or PRs being closed, or the contributor being blocked from the repository.
