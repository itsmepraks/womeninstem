## 💰 Scholarships and Funding

### Undergraduate Scholarships

#### **Society of Women Engineers (SWE) Scholarships**
- **Amount:** $1,000 - $15,000
- **Eligibility:** Women studying engineering or computer science
- **Deadline:** Typically February
- **Website:** [swe.org/scholarships](https://swe.org/scholarships/)
- **Notes:** Over 260 scholarships available; some are renewable. Both high school seniors and current college students can apply.

#### **Google Women Techmakers Scholars Program**
- **Amount:** $10,000 (US/Canada), €7,000 (Europe), other amounts globally
- **Eligibility:** Women in computer science or related technical field
- **Deadline:** Typically December
- **Website:** [buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com/scholarships/google-women-techmakers-scholars-program](https://buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com/scholarships/google-women-techmakers-scholars-program)
- **Notes:** Includes invitation to annual retreat. Open to undergraduate and graduate students.

#### **Palantir Women in Technology Scholarship**
- **Amount:** $10,000
- **Eligibility:** Women studying computer science or related field
- **Deadline:** Typically spring
- **Website:** [palantir.com/students/scholarship/wit-north-america](https://www.palantir.com/students/scholarship/wit-north-america/)
- **Notes:** Also includes networking opportunities with Palantir engineers.

#### **Microsoft Tuition Scholarships**
- **Amount:** Full tuition or partial tuition
- **Eligibility:** Various scholarships for different groups (women, minorities, students with disabilities)
- **Website:** [microsoft.com/university](https://www.microsoft.com/university)
- **Notes:** Multiple scholarship programs; check regularly for opportunities.

#### **BHW Women in STEM Scholarship**
- **Amount:** $3,000
- **Eligibility:** Women pursuing undergraduate or master's degree in STEM
- **Deadline:** Typically April
- **Website:** [thebhwgroup.com/scholarship](https://thebhwgroup.com/scholarship)
- **Notes:** Essay-based scholarship; focus on STEM career goals.

#### **Astronaut Scholarship Foundation**
- **Amount:** Up to $15,000
- **Eligibility:** Nominated by faculty; STEM majors
- **Website:** [astronautscholarship.org](https://www.astronautscholarship.org/)
- **Notes:** Prestigious award given at select universities.

#### **AAUW (American Association of University Women) Scholarships**
- **Amount:** Varies
- **Eligibility:** Women pursuing various STEM degrees
- **Website:** [aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants](https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/)
- **Notes:** Multiple programs for different career stages and needs.

#### **Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) Scholarships**
- **Amount:** $1,000 - $5,000
- **Eligibility:** Hispanic students in STEM
- **Website:** [shpe.org/students/scholarships](https://www.shpe.org/students/scholarships/)

#### **National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) Scholarships**
- **Amount:** Varies
- **Eligibility:** Black students in engineering
- **Website:** [nsbe.org/Programs/Scholarships.aspx](https://www.nsbe.org/Programs/Scholarships.aspx)

#### **Science Ambassador Scholarship (Full Ride)**
- **Amount:** Full tuition (up to $100,000)
- **Eligibility:** Women in STEM; video submission required
- **Website:** [scienceambassadorscholarship.org](https://www.scienceambassadorscholarship.org/)
- **Notes:** Unique video-based application process.

### Graduate Fellowships

#### **NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)**
- **Amount:** $37,000 annual stipend + $12,000 cost-of-education allowance (3 years)
- **Eligibility:** US citizens or permanent residents; STEM graduate students
- **Deadline:** October
- **Website:** [nsfgrfp.org](https://www.nsfgrfp.org/)
- **Notes:** Highly prestigious; can be used at any accredited US institution. Strong emphasis on broadening participation in STEM.

#### **Ford Foundation Fellowship Program**
- **Amount:** $27,000 (predoctoral), $29,000 (dissertation), $45,000 (postdoctoral)
- **Eligibility:** Underrepresented minorities and those committed to diversity
- **Website:** [nap.edu/ford](https://nap.edu/ford)
- **Notes:** Prestigious fellowship for advancing diversity in academia.

#### **Paul & Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans**
- **Amount:** $90,000 (over 2 years)
- **Eligibility:** Immigrants and children of immigrants in graduate programs
- **Website:** [pdsoros.org](https://www.pdsoros.org/)
- **Notes:** All fields, including STEM; highly competitive.

#### **Hertz Foundation Fellowship**
- **Amount:** Full tuition + $38,000 annual stipend (up to 5 years)
- **Eligibility:** Applied physical, biological, and engineering sciences
- **Website:** [hertzfoundation.org](https://www.hertzfoundation.org/)
- **Notes:** One of the most prestigious STEM fellowships.

#### **P.E.O. Scholar Awards**
- **Amount:** $20,000
- **Eligibility:** Women in final two years of doctoral program
- **Website:** [peointernational.org](https://www.peointernational.org/)
- **Notes:** International program with chapters worldwide.

#### **AAUW International Fellowships**
- **Amount:** $18,000 - $30,000
- **Eligibility:** International women studying in the US
- **Website:** [aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/international](https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/international/)

#### **L'Oréal USA For Women in Science Fellowship**
- **Amount:** $60,000
- **Eligibility:** Women postdoctoral researchers in life sciences, physical sciences, mathematics, engineering, computer science
- **Website:** [loreal.com/usa-forwomeninscience](https://www.loreal.com/usa-forwomeninscience)
- **Notes:** Prestigious award with strong visibility.

#### **Facebook Fellowship Program**
- **Amount:** Full tuition + stipend
- **Eligibility:** PhD students in computer science and related fields
- **Website:** [research.facebook.com/fellowship](https://research.facebook.com/fellowship)

#### **Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship**
- **Amount:** Full tuition + stipend
- **Eligibility:** PhD students in computing-related fields
- **Website:** [microsoft.com/research/academic-program/phd-fellowship](https://www.microsoft.com/research/academic-program/phd-fellowship)

#### **Google PhD Fellowship**
- **Amount:** Full tuition + stipend for 2-3 years
- **Eligibility:** PhD students in computer science
- **Website:** [research.google/outreach/phd-fellowship](https://research.google/outreach/phd-fellowship)
